Accuracy of Machine learning Models on Melbourne House Price Data



Datasets for Sydney and Melbourne were used in predicting prices by applying machine learning models. Linear regression, XGBoost and Randomforest Classifier was used in modelling and prediction. Random Forest and XGBoost produced better results with higher accuracy and low error compared to linear regression. Random Forest with higher accuracy was further used in prediction purpose.
